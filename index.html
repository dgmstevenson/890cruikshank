The Chalet

Mountain character, meet modern comfort.

Tucked onto sought-after Cruikshank Ridge, this custom-built chalet was designed for four-season mountain living. Soaring vaulted ceilings and warm fir trim give the great room a true alpine feel, while radiant in-floor heat over polished concrete keeps every corner cozy after a day on the hill.

The kitchen is finished with Merit cabinetry, granite counters and quality appointments — an easy place to gather a crowd. A flexible floor plan offers the potential for a fourth bedroom, so the chalet grows with your family and your guest list.

Out back, a generous southwest-facing deck is already wired and plumbed for a hot tub, framing long views across the Beaufort Range. This is a home that's ready to be enjoyed the day you get the keys.

The Location

Life at Mount Washington

Vancouver Island's biggest ski resort — and a year-round mountain playground — right outside your door.

❄️ Winter

  • 1,700+ acres and 505 m of vertical alpine terrain
  • A legendary ~9 metres of annual snowfall
  • 55 km of cross-country trails & a dedicated Nordic lodge
  • Tube park, snowshoeing and fat-bike trails

☀️ Summer

  • Lift-accessed mountain biking & a 2.3 km ZipTour
  • Scenic chairlift rides and alpine hiking
  • Disc golf, mini golf and family activities
  • Gateway to Strathcona Provincial Park

🍽️ Village & Community

  • Shops, dining and rentals in the alpine village
  • General store & everyday essentials on the mountain
  • A friendly, tight-knit owner community
  • Resort shuttle service from the valley

📍 Getting Here

  • ~25 km from the Comox Valley & Courtenay
  • Under an hour to Comox Valley Airport (YQQ)
  • Direct flights from Vancouver, Calgary & more
  • Beaches, wineries & the Comox Valley below
